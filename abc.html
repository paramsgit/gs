Driller For Underground Lines

Upto 250ft drill under roads for crossing of pipes for electricity wires. With 30 large drill bits, complete work of 5-6 days in just a few hours.

Modification.
Big tractors can be modified to make this drill machine for drill upto 150ft. For above 150 you need model GS25.
Efficency.
Can do 5-6 days of work in just a few hours. A powerful motor with high torque drive the drill bit through the ground. Sufficient power ensures efficient drilling performance, even in challenging soil or rock conditions
Drill Bit Options.
This machine offers a range of drill bit options suitable for drilling underground roads. Different bit designs may be required based on the soil or rock type encountered, such as auger bits, rock bits, or tunnel boring machine (TBM) cutters.

Hydraulic Loaders

Hydraulic front and back Loaders. Capacity 300kg to 1000kg (Depends on tractor model and size)

Compatibility.
Front loaders are designed to be compatible with specific tractor models, ensuring proper fit and attachment. Compatibility is crucial to ensure efficient operation and optimal performance of the loader.
Hydraulic Controls.
Very easy operatable with joysticks, allowing the operator to raise, lower, tilt, and maneuver the loader with ease.
ROPS.
ROPS (Roll-Over Protective Structure) is a protective structure designed to protect the operator in the event of a rollover accident. It enhances operator safety and is a crucial safety feature.
